We look forward to your visit. As you prepare for your adventure, we would like to share some important and helpful tips to ensure your upcoming escape is easy, relaxing and unforgettable.

How to Pack

  • Maine’s weather can be unpredictable. We recommend packing layers as temperatures change daily.
  • Be sure to pack a rain jacket. Terramor will have a selection of umbrellas available for complimentary use if needed.
  • Bring your camera! You’ll want to capture and remember the beauty of Maine this time of year.
  • We recommend you bring sunscreen, bug spray and water bottles if you plan on exploring Mount
    • Desert Island. If you forget something, don’t worry, we’ve got a store stocked with most essentials.

Important Information about Acadia National Park

If you are planning to visit Acadia during your time with us, there are some new changes to be aware of:
  • Vehicle reservations are required for Cadillac Summit Road during sunrise and daylight hours.
  • You can reserve your vehicle pass here
  • All visitors from May to October need to have a park entrance pass displayed in vehicle. There are limited places to purchase your pass in-person, we recommend purchasing a pass online prior to arrival.
  • You can purchase your Acadia park pass here
